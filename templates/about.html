About SkinSightAI

At SkinSightAI, we believe technology should empower better healthcare for everyone. Our mission is to make early skin disease detection accessible, fast, and reliable through cutting-edge Artificial Intelligence.

How It Works

Using advanced machine learning models, SkinSightAI analyzes skin images to predict possible conditions such as Acne, Nail Fungus, Skin Allergy, Hair Loss, and more. Simply upload an image or capture one live, and get instant, AI-powered insights about your skin health.

Our Vision

We envision a future where everyone has easy access to early skin diagnosis and preventive healthcare. Our goal is to bridge the gap between patients and specialists by providing preliminary guidance that saves time and effort.
